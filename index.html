Musk School of Space

The Musk School of Space is an elite school for Earth's best and brightest. Designed to help take humanity further than ever before and to propel us towards the future that sci-fi has dreampt up. The School was founded by Ryan Cabootee in 2093 just 3 years after he helped set up Moon Base Alpha. Named after the excentric billionare Elon Musk, who first concptualized a school like this, The Musck School of Space focuses on improving all aspects of students life through education and on making sure students are happy and healthy. Our school is a 6 year program starting at junior year of high school. Students are taught by the top scientists and astronauts wto prepare them for the vast unknown. Our aim is to help every student unlock their potential, to harness the potential of space and the universe around us. So if you are interested in helping further the spread of humanity in the cosmos and trailblaze the colonization of the galazy apply to The Musk School of Space.
