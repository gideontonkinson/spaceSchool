Travel

Here you can search flights to and from the moon so that you can book one for your next trip home to family, or trip back to school. If you have any trouble booking a flight reach out to Professor Teleteshanko and she can help you. If you have never travelled in space some of the largest companies are SpaceX, Nasa, Rocket Lab LTD, Astra Space and United Launch Alliance. Additonally you can leave the search bar empty to get the next 10 flights with any company.
