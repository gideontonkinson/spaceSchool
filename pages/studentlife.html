Student Life

Transport flights to the moon are offered provided free of charge by the Musk School of Space (up to 3 per year round trip). More infomation on flights can be found on the travel page for students who would like to schedule a trip at their convenience. Students will experience the highest quality commercial space craft in route to the moon. They will be able to enjoy and come to know their fellow classmates and learn from each other.
Each student is provided their own room and live in sections based on class year and schedule. Space suits, rovers, shuttle transportation all over the mooon, food, clothing and all other needs are provided by the school. Each room is outfitted with a bathroom, queen sized bed, desk, artificial light to grown plants, a 3D printer and a great view of the cosmos.

Classes

Our teachers love space and they love to teach about how to live here to their students. Our faculty is small but that combine with our small amount of students allows for a strong bond between them and students. That bond is needed and teaches trust and the need to rely on others in the depths of space where they are planning on going.
A variety of classe are offered here: Space Flight Training, Chemisty, Mechanical Engineering, Strength Training (required every semester because of the moons reduced gravity), Moon History, Space History, Computer Science, World History, Botany, English, Nutrition, Medical Training, Calculus, Physics, Stratigic Thinking and Analyzing the Accuracy of Space Films.
Each of these classes helps to round out students to be ready to advance into the unknown. Students also can ephasize and speak to their professor about what ephasizing in a certain aspect of space exploration would entail.
