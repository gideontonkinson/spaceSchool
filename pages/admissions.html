Admissions

Here at Musk School of Space, we accept only the top of the top. To apply you must take a series of tests and go through a thourough interview process. Here are the tests we require: Stress Test, IQ Test, Physical Health Exam and Test, Critical Thinking Exam, VisualizatRion Test, Spatial Reasoning Test, and a Personality Test.

Each year we get thousands of applications but we can only accept 25 new students per year. Year over year we have been able to accept more and more and hope to continue to do so. We additionally do accept 3 transfer students per year in school, so if you do not get in this year, please continue to apply.

If accepted all students ammenities, transportation to Moon Base Alpha, tuition, etc. are provided. We believe that what we do here is an investment in the future. We thank those who care about that future as much as we do an donate so that we can offer this experience.
